Character Profiles

The Beatcats are a group of five adorable singing kittens who just so happen to also be vampires that feast on the colours of their fan's excited hearts when they sing.

MIA

The pink Beatcat, and lead vocalist, Mia enjoys watching romcoms and girl's whose hearts are pounding, which gives her wild ideas. She has a hot and cold personality.

CHELSEA

The blue Beatcat, Chelsea enjoys electronics and VOCALOID music. Her comments can sometimes come off quite harsh, but they're usually well meaning.

RICO

The yellow Beatcat, Rico is not only a vampire like the others, but also a zombie. She is full of energy and enjoys dancing, running and exercise.

LAYLA

The purple Beatcat, Layla is always wearing headphones and in her own little world, tending to block out what's going on around her. Layla will sometimes perform raps on the songs.

EMMA

The green Beatcat, Emma is described by others as an airhead, something she doesn't agree with. She enjoys long baths and is able to dance elegantly.
